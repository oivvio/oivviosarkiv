As  part of my research for a book project on Allen Polite I did an
interview with Amiri Baraka in his home on February 23 2009. Polite and
Baraka first met in highschool i Newark, NJ. Later, as young adults,
they both moved to NYC, pursuing careers as writers. This unedited
interview is about 2 hours and 20 minutes long.
