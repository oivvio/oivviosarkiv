In late 2006 I made an interview with [Richard
Dyer](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Dyer) for Swedish national
radio. Professor Dyer was in Stockholm teaching a course on serial
killers in fiction at Stockholm University. I had him talk about his
book *White*, the state of critical whiteness studies, the connection
between fictional serial killers and whiteness. He also talks a bit
about his time at the
[CCCS](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Centre_for_Contemporary_Cultural_Studies),
his role in the founding of [The London Lesbian & Gay Film
Festival](http://www.llgff.org.uk/) and lots more. The version is pretty
much the raw unedited two hour interview.
